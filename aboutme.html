How did your team work together on the lightshow? How could your teamwork have improved?
-We tried passing around the robot Arduino around to each partner to try to figure out how to make the amount of LED lights given to us light up in a series of patterns. Every person tried a different way to position the resistors, jumper wires, LED lights so that the electricity could flow from one thing to the other. We could have taken much more time to ask each other questions pertaining to the project so that we could figure out the solution much quicker. That was needed so that rather than each person silently in their head trying to figure out the solution to the problem, the ENTIRE group could have understood what was actually going on, hahaha.
